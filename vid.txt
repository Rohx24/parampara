Protecting animals shows care for all life.
===

[00:00:00] On a quiet winter morning, a little bird was lost. A child noticed the bird and felt curious.

The little bird felt scared.

Grandma gently reminded the child to be careful. Little animals need kindness and space. The child learned to stay calm and quiet together. They helped the bird find its way home. The little bird was safe again, helping made the child feel proud and happy.

Protecting animals shows care for all life.
